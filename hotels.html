Hotels in South Korea

Tourists may be surprised to find very affordable places to stay called Goshitels and Goshiwons

Goshiwons are tiny, private living spaces primarily intended for students or young professionals who need affordable and convenient housing. These spaces are often characterized by their small size, with individual rooms usually equipped with a bed, desk, and basic amenities. Goshitels are similar but may offer slightly larger rooms with additional facilities like communal kitchens and bathrooms.
